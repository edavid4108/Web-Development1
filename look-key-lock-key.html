The founder of Look-Key Lock-Key, Mr. Troy Wesley, had a product that would save lives. Yes you heard that correct, save lives. But becuase of the amount of time needed to make this the perfect 'deadbolt', he needed someone to at least get the ball rolloing to create an electronic footprint, hint, a website. It would have to contain enough information and appeal to everyone, not just one target market. We're talking about safety here. So with that in mind, we were tasked to create a website layout that was both modern, but also convey this device was for everyone.

Starting from Scratch

While this product was still in the prototype stage, there were notes and emails, when pulled together, would be the foundation for the content. However there was no content to tell a story about safety. Instead of starting on the website, we drafted up several paragraphs to explain the reaosn of the Look-Key Lock-Key device. After many revisions, the content was ready, and we were ready to start laying out the website.
