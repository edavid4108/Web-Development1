FAYIN Insurance

-A Homepage Facelift

https://fayininsurance.com/

For all of your insurance needs, FAYIN has you covered! A local independent insurance agency was looking for a quick facelift of their homepage. After realizing that most of their clietns is from good ol' word of mouth, this modification would be somewhat quick and easy.

Starting the Process

Since we were concentrating only on the homepage, access to the administration area for WordPress was off limits (long story). So we were very limited on wahat we could change and add. We did notice that the main header was ediable (if that's a word). So we concentrated on creating simple carolsel banners with various messages about insurance.

We then concentrated on what we could change in the middle. Again, because of limitations that were imposed, we decided to at least bring out better their insurance partners (bottom part of screenshot), and to create a testimonie section that was discovered buried in the webiste, so we brought that out to where messages rotate from right to left (top part of screenshot).

So in a nushell a quick and effective project to bring FAYIN's homepage to a more modern standard.
