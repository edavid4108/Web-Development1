Jace & Mace Candle Company

-A Sweet Scent from Georgia!

www.jaceandmacecandleco.com

Jace & Mace is a startup company based in Atlanta Georgia. Inspired by the beautiful Tashay Reynolds, Jace & Mace was looking for both a logo, website, and other items that would promote thier brand. Her vision was something not too modern, but instead 'Southern'. We started with the basics. Logo first, then website. Wix was the platform of choice to host the website. With that being said, lets dig into the details.

Logos Here, Logos There...Logos Everywhere!

The first challenege was to come up with a logo which would was not too modern, but also not too old fassion. As you can see there were various layouts, colors, font types, blah, blah, blah. Take a look for yourself:

But there were two items that kept coming up: It needs to have a southern flavor and It needs to have wood somwhere in it. After several emails this is the final product:

And Now the Website...Wait...

Once the logo was finished, the design was pretty much straight forward. We decided to use Wix as not only the host for Jace & Mace, but also we decided to use their web builder application to build the website, as well as to handle all online purchases. We then decided to use a 'hint' of modren based fonts to stay within current trends. Okay, so we're done. Right? Not so fast. Becuase the candles were the same color, we felt that they would need to be identified some type of other way. So what was the final decision. Yep...more logos

Okay Now We're Ready

Back to the website. Overall it was three pages with a small section dedicated to the online storefront. The Wix application was very user friendly and robust. After several quality assurance tests from various people, the website finally went live. As for upkeep and maintenace, the site will be updated at least four time a year. JAce & MAce will normally release 3 new candle per season. Get them before they are gone!
