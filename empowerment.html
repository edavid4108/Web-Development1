Empowerment

We revived a First Church of God publicaton; making it ready for the digital world.

Empowerment is a publication exclusive to First Church of God in Columbus Ohio. Much like the vision that Bishop Timothy J Clarke, senior pastor at First Church of God, its a publication dedicated to uplifting people spiritually. But it's moer than that as well. Much like Bishop Clarke's belief, social justice is critical here in America. This publication also features how people would make this country a better place.
