Concept Web Design (Preview Here)

DRK Towing hails from Atlanta Georgia. A family owned towing company, they were looking to establish a electronic footprint on the internet. But they also were lookign for a logo as well. Nothing complecated, just something that they are out there. We were up to the task at hand.

Logos and Colors First, Website Second

DRK Towing already had a stock based logo on thei rbusiness card, but we wanted to give them the option to choose their own custom logo. Various designs were created to a. reflect their colors and b. they are a twoign company. Much like Jace & MAce Candle company, various designs came to mind. Below are the results:

On to the Concept

We tried to stay within the colors of DRK towing, so a basic black white color scheme was chosen. We also knew it had to be a no-nonsence design; hey you need a tow, why should you look at fluffy bunnies? So the layout is straigh and to the point; who we are, our phone number, and what we tow.
